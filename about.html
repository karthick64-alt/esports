About eSports Portal

The premier destination for competitive gaming excellence

Who We Are

eSports Portal is a leading platform dedicated to competitive gaming, bringing together players, teams, and fans from around the world. Founded in 2018, we've been at the forefront of the eSports revolution, hosting thousands of tournaments and connecting millions of gamers.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive ecosystem for eSports, offering tournament hosting, team management, live streaming, and community engagement tools that empower gamers to compete at the highest level.

Our History

2018 - The Beginning

eSports Portal was founded with a vision to revolutionize competitive gaming. We launched our first tournament with 100 participants.

2020 - Rapid Growth

Expanded to multiple game titles, reached 1 million registered users, and hosted our first $100,000 tournament.

2022 - Global Expansion

Launched mobile eSports division, partnered with major game developers, and reached 10 million active users worldwide.

2024 - Present Day

Continuing to innovate with AI-powered matchmaking, blockchain-based prize pools, and immersive viewing experiences. Over 25 million gamers trust our platform.
