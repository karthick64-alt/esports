Tournament Overview

The Valorant Championship 2024 is the premier competitive event for Valorant players worldwide. This tournament brings together the best teams from around the globe to compete for a massive prize pool of $250,000.

Featuring a double-elimination bracket format, teams will battle through qualifiers, group stages, and playoffs to determine the ultimate champion. All matches will be broadcast live with professional commentary and analysis.

Game Rules & Format

Tournament Format:

  • Qualifiers: Online single-elimination (March 20-25)
  • Group Stage: Round-robin groups of 4 (March 28-April 5)
  • Playoffs: Double-elimination bracket (April 10-20)
  • Grand Finals: Best of 5 series (April 21)

Game Rules:

  • Matches are Best of 3 (Bo3) except Finals (Bo5)
  • Map pool: Bind, Haven, Split, Ascent, Icebox, Breeze, Lotus
  • First team to 13 rounds wins the map
  • Overtime: First to 2 round wins
  • All agents and weapons are allowed

Team/Player Eligibility

  • Teams must have a minimum of 5 players and maximum of 7 (including substitutes)
  • All players must be at least 16 years old
  • Players must have a Valorant account ranked Diamond or above
  • Teams must be registered on eSports Portal
  • No player substitutions allowed after registration deadline
  • All players must be available for scheduled matches
  • Teams must have a designated captain/manager
  • Pro teams are welcome (no restrictions)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I register as an individual player?

No, this is a team tournament. You must register as part of a team with 5-7 players.

What happens if a player can't make a match?

Teams must use their registered substitutes. If no substitute is available, the match may be forfeited.

Is there a prize for spectators?

Premium Spectator tickets include access to exclusive content and giveaways during the tournament.

How are matches scheduled?

Matches are scheduled based on team availability and time zones. Teams will receive their schedule after registration closes.
